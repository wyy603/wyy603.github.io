Hello! I am Yuyang Wu (吴雨洋), a junior undergraduate in [Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences (IIIS, Yao Class)](https://iiis.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/) at Tsinghua University, specializing in Artificial Intelligence.

I was an undergraduate researcher at [3DVICI Lab, Tsinghua University](https://iiis.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/Research/Research_Groups/Laboratory_of_3D_Vision_Computing_and_Machine_Inte.htm), advised by Prof. [Yi Li](https://ericyi.github.io/). Currently I am a student intern at [CLIVR Lab, New York University](https://wp.nyu.edu/cilvr/), advised by Prof. [Sherry Yang](https://sherryy.github.io/index.html).

I have extensive experience in LLMs, Computer Vision, and Robotics, and I am eager to pursue further research and advanced studies in the field of Robotics in the future.
